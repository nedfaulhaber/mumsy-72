yitaku

Solar Flare from Auragami (2016)

Yitaku, hailing from Minneapolis, MN, is yet another example of the current importance of SoundCloud to the new music landscape. A home producer of electronic music, Yitaku has release 3 EP's in addition to joining a group of producers in their teens and early 20's called the Paper Crane Collective. The collective became a reality when young LA producer Madnap began reaching out to other SoundCloud musicians whom he liked. Eventually, a network developed and the collective expanded from a few people to a group of about a dozen producers from all over the country, like-minded but representing a diverse spectrum of electronic music.

After 3 compilations of songs by the varying members (Volumes 1-3), Paper Crane released their 4th feature called Auragami in 2016. With its large following on SoundCloud, Madnap was able to put together a short tour with stops in LA and New York to present events and debut their new line of clothing and other merchandise that the group collaborated on creating. Self-described now as a Music / Event Series / Community / Merch collective, Paper Crane continues to exhibit their forward-thinking, entrepeneurial spirit, presenting events around the LA area and beyond.
